# Configuring UpCloud API Access

## Step 1

Log in to your UpCloud Control Panel at [hub.upcloud.com](https://hub.upcloud.com)

&nbsp;

## Step 2

Navigate to **People** â†’ **Accounts** in the left sidebar

&nbsp;

## Step 3

### Create a Subaccount (Recommended)

Click **Create subaccount** to create a dedicated account for API access

&nbsp;

## Step 4

Fill in the subaccount details:
- **Username**: A descriptive name (e.g., "massdriver-api")
- **Password**: A strong password
- **Email**: Contact email for the account

&nbsp;

## Step 5

### Assign Permissions

Grant the subaccount appropriate permissions:
- **Allow API connections**: Must be enabled
- **Server access**: Select the servers/resources the account can manage
- **Other permissions**: Configure based on your needs

&nbsp;

## Step 6

Click **Create** to save the subaccount

&nbsp;

## Step 7

Alternatively, you can use your main account credentials, but a subaccount is recommended for security and audit purposes.

&nbsp;

## Step 8

Fill in the form with:
- **Username**: The UpCloud username (main account or subaccount)
- **Password**: The account password

&nbsp;

## Step 9

Click **Create** and head to the [projects page](/projects) to start building your infrastructure.
