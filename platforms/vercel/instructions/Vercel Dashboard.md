# Creating a Vercel API Token

## Step 1

Log in to your Vercel account at [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)

&nbsp;

## Step 2

Navigate to **Account Settings** → **Tokens** or go directly to [vercel.com/account/tokens](https://vercel.com/account/tokens)

&nbsp;

## Step 3

Click **Create** to generate a new token

&nbsp;

## Step 4

Give your token a descriptive name (e.g., "Massdriver Provisioning")

&nbsp;

## Step 5

Select the appropriate scope:
- **Full Account** for personal projects
- **Specific Team** if deploying to a team

&nbsp;

## Step 6

Set an expiration date or select "No Expiration" for long-lived tokens

&nbsp;

## Step 7

Click **Create Token** and copy the generated token immediately (it won't be shown again)

&nbsp;

## Step 8

Paste the token into the **API Token** field in the form

&nbsp;

## Step 9

If deploying to a team, find your Team ID in **Team Settings** → **General** and paste it into the **Team ID** field

&nbsp;

## Step 10

Click **Create** and head to the [projects page](/projects) to start building your infrastructure.
